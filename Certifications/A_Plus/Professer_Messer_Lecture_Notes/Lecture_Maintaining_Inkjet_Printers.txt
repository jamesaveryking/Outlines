Maintaining Inkjet Printers

-cleaning print heads
	-small droplets of ink
	-clogging is common
	-clogged heads is an issue -- automatic cleaning
	-can be manually cleaned
	-some print heads or cartridges can be removed
	-can clean with isopropyl alcohol
-replacing inkjet cartridges
	-usually separate colors -- CMYK
	-some cartridges combine
	-cartridges are proprietary
	-mostly plastic and need to be recycled
-inkjet printer calibration
	-align nozzles to paper
	-may need to make minor adjustments to tune
-clearing jams
	-remove paper tray -- any paper that is loose
	-check for any paper scraps
