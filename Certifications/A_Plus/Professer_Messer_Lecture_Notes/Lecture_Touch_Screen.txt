Overview of Input and Output Devices
-touch screen
	-no keyboard
	-on-screen input and output
	-many different examples
-KVM -- keyboard, video, mouse
	-use many computers with a single keyboard, video display, and mouse
-Smart TV
	-apps on a television
	-streaming media, telecommunications
	-input -- remote, keyboard, voice
	-output -- display
	-connectivity
		-Wired Ethernet
		-802.11 wireless
-Set-top box
	-add intelligence to less intelligent televisions
	-potentially connect to computer to stream media
	-wired ethernet, 802.11 wireless, HDMI
-MIDI -- musical instrument ddigital interface
	-industry standard since 1983
	-traditional uses 5-pin DIN
	-modern use USB, ethernet, firewire
