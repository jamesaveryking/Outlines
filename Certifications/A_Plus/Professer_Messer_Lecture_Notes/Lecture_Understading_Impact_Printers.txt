Understanding Impact Printers

-dot-matrix is one of the more common
	-presses against a ribbon to make a mark on a paper
	-good for multiple carbon copies
	-low cost per page
	-print head with small matrix of pins
	-cons: noisy with poor graphics
	-niche use: car rentals, airports
	-printer head
		-one matrix must move across page to print
	-printer ribbon -- fabric, one long ribbon, never ending circle
		-easy to replace -- one single unit
		-proprietary size -- check to optimize
	-tractor feed
		-paper pulled through with holes on side of paper
		-continuous paper feed
		-holes need to align exactly
	-paper can be perforated on sides to remove after print
