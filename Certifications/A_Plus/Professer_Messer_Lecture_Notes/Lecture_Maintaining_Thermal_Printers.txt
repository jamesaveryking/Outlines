Maintaining Thermal Printers

-thermal paper replacement
	-relatively inexpensive
	-different sizes
	-acutal replacement is easy -- simple paper feed
-cleaning heating element
	-liquid cleaner -- isopropyl alcohol on cleaning pen
	-swab gently to avoid other electrical components
	-potentially use cleaning card to clean path
-removing debris
	-relatively small amount -- take outside and blow it out
	-avoid using a vacuum -- static buildup and discharge
	-wipe it out with a damp cloth
