Maintaining Impact Printers

-printer ribbon replacement
	-single ribbon, self-contained, one long circle
	-replace when ink becomes too light -- ink eventually consumed
	-designed to be modular
-print head replacement
	-known to be versatile
	-gets hot
	-another modular component
	-replace print head and ribbon at same time
-replacing paper
	-not as easy as a laser printer
	-paper must feed perfectly into available holes
	-forms must be positioned correctly to use predefined space
